I am now working on my master's thesis in real-work 3D gaze tracking at [Computer Vision Lab](https://vision.ee.ethz.ch), supervised by [Nikola Popvic](https://www.linkedin.com/in/nikola-popovic-172252113/), [Xi Wang](https://xiwang1212.github.io/homepage/), and [Danda Pani Paudel](https://people.ee.ethz.ch/~paudeld/). Previously I worked on  Neural Radiance Field for multi-scene setting at [Autonomous System Lab](https://asl.ethz.ch), supervised by [Kenneth Blomqvist](https://keke.dev) and [Francesco Milano](https://www.linkedin.com/in/francesco-milano-ba5483132/).

Before my master's study in Zurich, I also enrolled remotely at Carnegie Mellon University as a [Mechanical Engineering](https://www.meche.engineering.cmu.edu) master's student for 2 semesters, during which I took the great [11-785 Introduction to Deep Learning](https://deeplearning.cs.cmu.edu/S21/index.html) course, which opened up the fascinating world of machine intelligence to me.  

My research interest mainly lies in the intersection of 3D vision and deep learning. Motivated by my thesis on gaze tracking, I am now leaning toward more human-centered side of these fields.
